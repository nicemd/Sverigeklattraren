# Guide content licence

Sverigeklättraren deliberately separates freely reusable climbing knowledge
from the proprietary website used to present and maintain it.

guide content is distributed under the **GNU Free Documentation License,
Version 1.3**:

- the preserved 2014 MediaWiki guide snapshot in `mediawiki/`;
- publishable guide records in `content/areas.json` and `content/areas/`;
- guide descriptions, route and problem descriptions, beta, sector text,
  structured guide data, and committed translations derived from that content;
- committed guide-content enrichment in `content/enrichment/`; and
- images and topos in `images/` when their source or provenance identifies
  them as licensed content from the guide archive.

The complete, unmodified licence text is included in
[`LICENSE-CONTENT-GFDL-1.3.txt`](LICENSE-CONTENT-GFDL-1.3.txt).

Translations and editorial rewrites of GFDL material are modified versions of
that material and remain under the GFDL. Their source and modification history
is preserved through provenance records and Git history.

Version 1.3. Facts taken from an external source do not grant permission to
copy that source's wording, images, or topos.

## Other source licences

An external source or individual media item with explicit licence metadata
keeps that licence. A `fact-reference` provenance entry identifies a factual
source only and does not authorize republication of its protected expression
or media. The per-source metadata is authoritative when it differs from the
default above.
